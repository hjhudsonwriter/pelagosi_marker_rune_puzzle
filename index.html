Ritual Instructions

How the trial works

  1. Study the spiral and determine which rune is missing from the broken socket.
  2. Place that rune into the Marker to restore the pattern.
  3. When the base runes awaken, press them in the remembered ocean order.
  4. A wrong activation triggers a sea-surge and resets the ritual.

Your aim is simple: restore the broken spiral, then awaken the Marker correctly.
